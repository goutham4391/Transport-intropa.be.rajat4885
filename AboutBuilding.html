Intropa Building

Our headquarters are centrally located in the city of Antwerp in the prestigious Intropa Building. The Intropa Building is on the Quellinstraat, a highly presentable street adjacent to the city park and two blocks away from Antwerp’s Central Station. The street has seen exceptional developments in recent years, including the opening of Antwerp’s new and prominent post-office, the modern renovation of the Hylit hotel and the artful facelifts of historic buildings that exude a distinctive charm.

Each floor in the south facing Intropa Building spans 263 square meters of airy and open space, with large windows that draw in beams of light. The entrance hall boasts double high ceilings and the building car park accommodates 37 car spaces. With CCTV security, key card access and a dedicated concierge on site, the Intropa Building guarantees comfort and offers the perfect home for your business.
